Compare the reading level of two texts

Have you ever find yourself in a situation where you wanted to explain what a word means to a child, but so many definitions came to your mind that you could not choose the most suitable one? With this web app, no more situations like this, just enter the two definitions you want to compare and it will return you to the one with the easiest reading level.

Since I used a smaller version of bert for deployment, the performance of the model used in this app is not as good as the ones I created during the competition.
